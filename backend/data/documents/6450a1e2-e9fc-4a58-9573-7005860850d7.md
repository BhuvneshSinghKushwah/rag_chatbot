# Shipping Information

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 (Free on orders over $50)
- Available: All 50 US states

### Express Shipping
- Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99
- Available: Continental US only

### Next-Day Shipping
- Delivery Time: 1 business day
- Cost: $24.99
- Order by 2 PM local time for next-day delivery
- Available: Major metropolitan areas only

### Same-Day Delivery
- Delivery Time: Within 4 hours
- Cost: $14.99
- Order by 12 PM local time
- Available: Select cities (New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, San Jose, Dallas, Austin, Seattle, Denver)

## International Shipping
We ship to over 100 countries worldwide.

### Canada
- Standard: 7-10 business days ($15.99)
- Express: 3-5 business days ($29.99)

### Europe
- Standard: 10-14 business days ($24.99)
- Express: 5-7 business days ($49.99)

### Asia-Pacific
- Standard: 14-21 business days ($29.99)
- Express: 7-10 business days ($59.99)

Note: International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes. These fees are the responsibility of the recipient.

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2 PM PST on business days are processed same day
- Orders placed after 2 PM PST or on weekends/holidays are processed next business day
- Processing time does not include shipping transit time

## Tracking Your Order
1. You'll receive a confirmation email when your order ships
2. Click the tracking link in the email
3. Or log into your account and view "Order Status"
4. Track packages via our website, app, or carrier website

## Shipping Restrictions
Some items cannot be shipped to certain locations:
- Lithium batteries: Cannot ship via air freight
- Oversized items: Additional shipping charges may apply
- Hazardous materials: Ground shipping only, continental US

## Delivery Instructions
You can add delivery instructions during checkout:
- Leave at door
- Require signature
- Deliver to neighbor
- Hold at carrier facility

## P.O. Box Delivery
- Standard shipping available to P.O. boxes
- Express and same-day shipping require street address

## APO/FPO Addresses
We proudly ship to military addresses:
- Standard shipping: 10-14 business days
- Cost: $5.99 (Free on orders over $50)

## Lost or Damaged Packages
If your package is lost or arrives damaged:
1. Contact us within 7 days of expected delivery
2. We'll file a claim with the carrier
3. Replacement or refund issued within 48 hours of claim approval
