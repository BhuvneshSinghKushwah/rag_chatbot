# Return Policy

## Overview
At TechGadgets Inc., we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward return process.

## Return Window
- Standard products: 30 days from delivery date
- Electronics: 15 days from delivery date
- Sale items: 14 days from delivery date (final sale items are non-returnable)

## Eligibility Requirements
To be eligible for a return, items must be:
- Unused and in original condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Perishable goods
- Intimate or sanitary products
- Hazardous materials

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Log into your account at techgadgets.com
2. Navigate to "Order History"
3. Select the order containing the item you wish to return
4. Click "Request Return" and follow the prompts
5. Print the prepaid shipping label (for orders over $50)
6. Pack the item securely and drop off at any authorized shipping location

## Refund Process
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

## Exchanges
If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or color:
1. Return the original item following the steps above
2. Place a new order for the desired item
3. We'll prioritize processing your new order

## Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
- Email photos of the damage to support@techgadgets.com
- We'll send a replacement at no additional cost
